The objectives of the programmes offered by the Department of Information Technology are:

1. To equip students with state of art knowledge and skill in Information Technology and to produce engineers with outstanding ability.

2. To create future leaders of IT industry and profession.

3. To add to the existing intellectual pool of young minds to meet the growing demands of the nation in Information Technology and

4. To lay a foundation for life long learning ability and a capacity for adaptation in the ever changing world.
